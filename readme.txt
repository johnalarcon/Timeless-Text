Texts like "I have worked for n years in..." or "with n years experience, I have been..." are outdated within a year. This plugin keeps these types of time-sensitive texts up to date automatically.

== Description ==


# Why make timeless the text?
If you have 12 years of experience, your site shouldn't say you only have 10! But, will you remember to update it each year? Yeah, me neither. This problem is compounded for teams – if your team of 4 have 20 years of combined experience, that number will be shortchanged by 4 years for every year that you forget to update it!

# Simple Shortcodes
There are no complicated settings, no hard-to-find menus. Simply add a shortcode to any post or page (or anywhere else you have enabled shortcodes) and forget about it!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

# Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I suppress the text part and just use the number?
Yes. See the documentation.

### Can this plugin handle cumulative values?
Yes. Cumulative values are useful for texts like, "Our staff has n years of combined experience." You will find instructions in the documentation.

== Installation ==

# Manual Installation

- **Download** the zip file to your local computer
- **Login** to your ClassicPress website.
- **Navigate** to `Dashboard > Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin`
- **Upload** the zip file to your site
- Click to **Install** the plugin
- Click to **Activate** the plugin
